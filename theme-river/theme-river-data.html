This theme river visualizes job placements by area of assignment for Library and Information Science graduates over time.

I compiled the data from LibraryJournal's Placements and Salaries survey (2011-2015). Each year reflects the placements of the previous year's graduates - e.g. the 2015 data reflect job placements of 2014 graduates.

In 2015, LibraryJournal asked for job title rather than area of job assignment. Wherever possible, I mapped the job title responses onto the job areas from 2011-2014. You can view the compiled data here.
