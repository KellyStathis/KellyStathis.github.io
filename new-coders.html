About the data

This data comes from the 2016 New Coder Survey conducted by Free Code Camp, and created by Quincy Larson and Saron Yitbarek. The survey had 15,620 responses to 43 questions; some questions were asked only as a follow-up, and none were required. Of the 43 questions, 22 were demographic questions and the rest related to coding.
